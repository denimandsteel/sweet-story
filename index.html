A Sweet Story

Have you ever wanted to
dance with a donut?

Did you ever
wish you could joke
around with a jellybean?

Oliver Pumpernickel has done both! Oliver is a little boy with a big sweet tooth. He loves sweets so much that he daydreams about them all day long. Join Oliver as he goes on adventures with his own unique, sweet brand of special friends.

A new interactive ebook for kids aged 4-8.

We love books. We know you do too. It’s time for an original children’s ebook that marries the quality of traditional narrative storytelling with the fun of modern interactivity. Come and see why reading A Sweet Story takes story time to new heights. Inspire your child’s imagination, arouse their curiosity, and empower them to participate in this beautifully illustrated adventure tale.

Available for the iPad just in time for the holidays!

“Childhood is a special time. The world is new and almost everything is completely amazing. A Sweet Story is my contribution to all the childhoods of today. It was inspired by my own kids who, like most children, know what they like and just want it all the time! Grown ups can be so serious. Kids know the truth: Life is better when it’s sweet.”

—Jono Howard, author

Author

Jono Howard is a Leo-nominated writer and Story Editor who has worked in the animation industry for over 17 years. Based in Vancouver, he has written over a hundred produced scripts for numerous animated children's series shown on networks around the world, among them MTV, ABC, CBS, Nickelodeon, and ITV (United Kingdom), including a five season stint as Head Writer for Cartoon Network’s long-running original series Ed, Edd n’ Eddy. He also Co-created (with pal Jon Izen) and Story Edited the CBC original series The Very Good Adventures of Yam Roll in Happy Kingdom!

Production, Direction & Design

Global Mechanic Media works to add more thought and beauty to the world. Founded in 2000 by Bruce Alcock, Global Mechanic Media is a creative studio that makes films, commercials, TV series and digital media. Drawing on decades of design and production, A Sweet Story is Global Mechanic Media's debut in the growing world of interactive ebooks.

Coding & Development

Kris & Brake Technologies Inc develop mobile applications and engaging educational software for the modern family. Originally founded in 2010 as a partnership between close friends Kristopher Goudie & E Brake, K&B technologies has expanded to a budding office with over 40 years combined experience creating interactive software for international clients. A Sweet Story is the exciting and innovative culmination of this expanding and family-oriented team.
